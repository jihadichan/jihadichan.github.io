在宽广的海底，有一个世界叫海底王国。这个王国在大海深处，是连锚都不能到达的海底深处。深到陆地上的人们也不可能到访。
At the bottom of the wide ocean is a world called the Sea Kingdom. This kingdom lies at the deepest part of the ocean, deeper than any anchor can go. It is so deep that humans who live on land can never visit.
在海底王国，到处都是蓝色的海水。那里的水像海面的波浪一样起伏。当大海很平静时，阳光照到这个深处。在安静的夜晚，月亮和星星发出的白光会在水中闪闪发光。
In the Sea Kingdom, blue water covers everything. The water there moves like the waves above. When the ocean is calm, the sun’s rays reach down to this deep place. On a still night, white light from the moon and stars sparkles in the water.
海底生长着最美丽的树。有的树有紫色的树枝和绿色的叶子。那里还有五颜六色的花园。植物都随着波浪慢慢地摇摆。
The most beautiful trees grow at the bottom of the sea. Some have purple branches and green leaves. There are gardens with many brightly colored flowers too. The plants all slowly sway with wave.
各种大大小小的鱼在叶子和花儿之间游来游去。它们整天玩儿，睡在岩石之间。
人鱼们住在海底王国里，他们有男的也有女的。
Many kinds of fish, large and small, swim among the leaves and the flowers. They play all day and sleep in cracks in rocks.
Merpeople live in the Sea Kingdom, and there are mermen and mermaids.
人鱼们喜欢跟鱼朋友们一起玩儿。美人鱼也喜欢用她们美丽的双手喂鱼。像鱼儿朋友们一样，人鱼们也有尾巴帮他们游泳。人鱼们爱大海和里面的一切。
Merpeople love to play with their fish friends. The mermaids also love to feed the fish from their pretty hands. Like their fish friends, the merpeople have tails to help them swim. The merpeople love the ocean and everything in it.
他们从来没想过去别的地方生活。他们能活很长时间，大概三百年。人鱼们在花园里唱歌、玩儿，幸福地度过每一天。
They have never thought about going somewhere else to live. And they live a very long time—about three hundred years. The merpeople are happy to spend their days singing and playing in the gardens.
很久以前，一个国王住在大海最深处的一座王宫里。他和他的六个女儿生活在一起。他的王宫有亮橙色的珊瑚墙。黑色的贝壳覆盖着屋顶。
A long time ago, a king lived in a palace at the deepest part of the sea. He lived with his six young daughters. His palace bright orange coral walls. Black clams covered the roof.
黑色贝壳随着潮水的变化开合。贝壳打开时，里面都会有一颗发光的银色珍珠。
海底国王很善良，统治着水底的所有生物。
The black clams opened and closed with the changing of the tide. Whenever a shell was open, there was a silver pearl inside.
The Sea King was kind and ruled over all the creatures underwater.
他希望大家都幸福。但是，他过得很伤心。
“我好伤心，我的妻子——王后，很多年前去世了。” 他叹了口气，“现在，我一个人照顾六个女儿。” 
He wanted everyone to be happy, but he was living in sadness.
“How sad that my wife, the Queen, died long ago.” He sighed. “Now I care for my six daughters all by myself.”
“不，你不是一个人。” 他的妈妈——智慧人鱼说，“我也在照顾她们。还有我让这个王宫保持整洁、干净。儿子，你总是忘记我在这里做的一切！” 
“No, you're not alone,” said his mother, the Wise Mermaid. “I take care of them too. And I'm keeping the palace clean and tidy. Son, you always forget about everything I do around here!”
“您说的对，妈妈。” 海底国王笑着说，“这个家幸亏有您。” 
智慧人鱼对整个世界很了解。她把她的孙女们教得很好。“等你们准备好了，” 她总是这样告诉公主们，“我会告诉你们关于地上世界的生活。” 
“You are right, Mother,” said the Sea King with a smile. “This family is very lucky that you are here.”
The Wise Mermaid knew a lot about every world. And she taught her granddaughters well. “When you are ready, I will teach you about life in the Upper World,” she always told the princesses.
姐妹们各差一岁。当每个公主满十五岁时，她就可以游到海面上去。那时，那个公主就第一次看到地上世界。
The sisters were each born one year apart. When each princess turned 15, she could swim up to the sea surface. That was the first time the princess would see the Upper World.
公主们觉得海底王国的生活简单、幸福。她们每天都很开心，笑声不断。可是，最小的妹妹不一样。她很安静，爱思考，有点独特。因为她是年纪最小、身材最小的，大家都叫她小美人鱼。
The princesses felt happy with their simple lives in the Sea Kingdom. Every one of their days was spent in fun and laughter. But the youngest princess was different. That she was quiet and thoughtful was a bit unusual. Because she was the youngest and the smallest, they called her the Little Mermaid.
每个公主都拥有自己的小花园。一个公主把她的花园做成了鲸鱼的样子。另一个公主把她的花园做成了贝壳的样子。公主们在花园里放满了她们在沉船里找到的奇怪东西。
Each princess had her own small flower garden. One princess made her flower garden in the shape of a whale. Another princess made her garden in the shape of a shell. The princesses filled their gardens with odd things they had found in sunken ships.
可是，小美人鱼的花园很简单。只有红色的花和一个人类男孩雕像。那个雕像是她妈妈的，小美人鱼很爱它。
But the Little Mermaid’s garden was simple. It had only red flowers and a statue of a human boy. The statue had been her mother’s, and the Little Mermaid loved it.
当姐姐们在她们花园里玩儿的时候，小美人鱼静静地坐在她的花园里。看着那个男孩雕像，她对人类和地上世界充满了好奇。
“地上世界是什么样子的？” 有一天，她问奶奶。
When her sisters played in their flower gardens, the Little Mermaid sat quietly in hers. Looking at the statue of the boy, she grew curious about what humans and the Upper World were like.
“What is the Upper World like?” she asked her grandmother one day.
“哦，小宝贝，那里有很多值得看的！” 智慧人鱼说，“那里有树林，当空气变冷时，就会变颜色。很快，树叶就会掉到地上。然后，就会下雪。” 
“Oh, little one, there is so much to see!” said the Wise Mermaid. “There are forests, and when the air turns cold, they change colors. Then, not long after, their leaves fall to the ground. Then snow comes.”
“雪？” 小美人鱼说，“雪是什么？” 
“雪是由小冰花组成的。” 奶奶回答，“在冬天，这些小冰花从天上落下来。但是在夏天，绿草覆盖土地，到处充满了浓郁的花香。” 
“Snow?” asked the Little Mermaid. “What’s snow?”
“Snow is made of small ice flakes,” replied her grandmother. “In winter these ice flakes fall from the sky. But in summer green grass covers the ground, and the smell of flowers is everywhere.”
“听起来很棒。” 小美人鱼说，“在这里，我们的树没有变化，花也没有香味。” 
智慧人鱼告诉小美人鱼更多的东西。“在地上世界，你可以仰望天空，清楚地看见太阳、月亮和星星。小鸟在树之间飞来飞去，唱着好听的歌。” 
“It sounds wonderful,” the Little Mermaid said. “Here our trees never change, and the flowers have no smell at all.”
The Wise Mermaid let the Little Mermaid know more. “In the Upper World, you can look up in the sky and clearly see the sun, moon, and stars. Birds fly amid the trees, singing lovely songs.”
“给我讲讲动物吧，奶奶。” 小美人鱼恳求说。
“就像我说的那样，有些动物飞。但是，很多动物有四条腿，在地上走。人类用两条腿走，他们非常擅长造东西。他们盖了很多房子，房子组成了村庄。” 
“Tell me about the animals, Grandmother,” the Little Mermaid begged.
“Some animals fly, as I said. But many have four feet and walk on the ground. Humans walk on two feet, and they are very good at making things. They build lots of houses, and the houses form towns.”
小美人鱼想听得更多。“他们也会造船，是吧？” 
“哦，是的，小宝贝。” 奶奶说，“不过，你已经知道了。” 
The Little Mermaid wanted to hear more. “They also build ships, don’t they?”
“Oh yes, little one,” her grandmother said. “But you already know that.”
“我知道，但是我想听关于人类的事情。他们在上面航海，离我们很近。可是，他们的世界看起来很遥远。” 
“是的，人类跟我们很不一样。” 奶奶说，“他们不像人鱼这么长寿。我们能活三百年！但是，人类有灵魂。” 
“I know, but I want to hear about humans. They come so close to us as they sail above. But their world seems so far away.”
“Yes, humans are very different from us,” said her grandmother. “They don’t live as long as merpeople. We live for three hundred years! But humans have souls.”
“灵魂是什么？” 小美人鱼问。
“我也不知道，小宝贝。” 奶奶回答，“听说灵魂能永生。但是，人类也不真正了解灵魂是什么。不管它是什么，我觉得它一定很特别。” 
“What is a soul?” the Little Mermaid asked.
“I don’t know either, little one,” her grandmother replied. “I have heard a soul can live forever. But humans don’t really know what a soul is either. Whatever it is, I think it must be a very special thing.”
“我想了解人类的一切！” 
“等你十五岁了，你会知道的。” 奶奶说，“你能游到海面上去，看有些船来来往往。” 
“可是，我想现在就去！” 小美人鱼恳求说。
“I want to know everything about humans!”
”When you are 15 years old, you will find out,” said her grandmother. “You’ll swim to the surface and you will be able to watch the ships sail by.”
“But I want to go now!” the Little Mermaid pleaded.
“你要有耐心。再过五年，就轮到你了。” 奶奶笑着说，“不过，你会发现，宝贝，我们海底的家比别的任何地方都好。” 
几年过去了，姐姐们都去过地上世界。但是，她得等待。
“Be patient. In five years, your turn will come,” said her grandmother, smiling. “But you will find, dear, that our home here under the sea is better than any place else.”
Years passed and the Little Mermaid’s sisters went to the Upper World. But she had to wait.
在晴朗的夜晚，她抬头看着月亮和星星。当一个阴影掠过她们时，她知道那是一艘船从她们头顶开过去。小美人鱼渴望地把胳膊伸向船底。总有一天，她将触摸到地上世界。
So on clear nights, she looked up at the moon and stars. Whenever a shadow passed across them, she knew it was a ship passing overhead. The Little Mermaid longingly stretched her white arms up toward the bottom of the ship. One day she too would touch the Upper World.
每个公主到十五岁生日时，她就可以游上去，看到地上世界。她会告诉姐妹们她看到了什么。
When each princess reached her fifteenth birthday, she could swim to the Upper World. She would tell the others what she saw.
当最大的公主满十五岁时，她在海边呆了一阵子。“我躺在月光下的沙滩上，望着一个远远的城市。” 她告诉妹妹们，“那些房子灯光闪烁，铃声隐隐地从高塔上传来。” 
When the eldest princess turned 15, she spent time near the sea. “I lay on the beach under the moonlight and looked at a distant town,” she told her sisters. “The houses’ lights twinkled, and bells rang softly from a tall tower.”
第二年，第二个公主在日落时分游了上去。“我看见了金色的海水和红色的天空。” 她说，“我看见了软绵绵的紫色云彩，高高地挂在我头上。还有海面上一群白天鹅飞向夕阳。” 
The next year the second sister came up to the surface at sunset. “I saw golden water and a red sky,” she said. “I saw fluffy purple clouds hanging high overhead. And a flock of white swans flew toward the setting sun.”
下一年，第三个公主更加深入了地上世界。“我沿着河游泳，看见了起伏的山岭和有着高大绿树的树林。我听见鸟儿在给孩子们唱幸福的歌。我还看到了没有尾巴的人类在海里游泳！” 
The following year the third sister went deeper into the Upper World. “I swam up a river and saw rolling mountains and woods filled with tall, green trees. I heard birds sing happy songs to their chicks. I even saw humans swimming in the sea without tails!”
第三个公主被人类吓得游回了宽广的大海。听了这件事，第四个公主失去了勇气。她一直离陆地很远，只呆在汹涌的波涛里。
Frightened of the humans, the third sister swam back to the open sea.
Hearing this, the fourth sister lost her courage. She kept far away from land and stayed in the rough waves.
“我看见调皮的海豚在海面上跳上跳下。” 她对姐妹们说，“巨大的鲸鱼从背上的洞里喷水。远远地，我看见了很多船。船离得很远，看起来像海鸥。” 
"I saw playful dolphins jumping up and down in the water,” she told her sisters. “Huge whales sprayed water through holes in their backs. In the distance I saw many ships. They were so far away that they looked like seagulls.”
现在轮到第五个了。她的生日在冬天。冬天时，大海会变成深绿色，而且暴风雨猛烈。巨大的冰山浮出了水面。
Now it was the fifth sister's turn. Her birthday was in winter. In winter the sea often turned dark green and storms were strong. Enormous icebergs stuck out of the water.
“我坐在一座冰山上，看见一艘船经过。” 第五个公主对姐姐说，“很快，大海变得波涛汹涌，绿色的波涛变成了黑色。雷声轰鸣，我看见闪电划过了天空！” 
“I sat on an iceberg, and watched a ship pass by,” she told her sisters. “Soon the waves became rough and the green waves turned black. Thunder roared, and I saw lightning flash in the sky!”
一天晚上，五个姐姐们一起游到了海面上。她们坐在岩石上，向船唱歌。她们的声音很美丽，响彻天空。但是，当船员听见姐妹们的歌声时，他们很害怕。
Some evenings the five older sisters swam up to the surface together. They sat on rocks and sang to the ships. Their voices beautifully rang out to the sky. But when the sailors heard the sisters’ songs, they became frightened.
船员们马上改变船的方向。几年过去了，人们开始讲唱歌人鱼的故事。他们说人鱼们很邪恶，因为人鱼想欺骗人类。
The sailors quickly changed the direction of their ships. As the years went by, humans told stories of the singing merpeople. They said merpeople were wicked because they tricked humans.
他们还说愚蠢的船员们喜欢听人鱼们唱歌，会把船开得很近。然后，他们的船会撞到岩石。
但这些故事都不是真的。姐妹们不想伤害任何人。她们只是爱唱歌而已！
They said that foolish sailors loved to hear the merpeople's songs and would sail their boats close. Then their ships would crash into rocks.
None of these stories were true. The sisters did not want to hurt anyone. They just loved to sing!
五个公主最后对地上世界失去了兴趣。她们越来越少地向船唱歌了。对人鱼来说，没有地方比得上他们海底的家。海底王国永远都是最适宜居住的地方。
The five princesses eventually lost interest in the Upper World. And they spent less and less time singing to the ships. For the merpeople, there was no place as nice as their home at the bottom of the sea. The Sea Kingdom would always be the best place to live.
五年里，小美人鱼听姐姐们讲故事。她渴望自己到地上世界的冒险。
“哦，好希望我已经十五岁了。” 她渴望地说，“我会爱上地上世界和那里的人类！” 
For five years the Little Mermaid listened to her sisters’ tales. She longed for an adventure in the Upper World.
“Oh, how great would it be if I were 15,” she said. “I know I would love the Upper World and all the humans in it!”
终于，小美人鱼十五岁的生日到了。
“今天你终于能看见地上世界了。” 奶奶说。
“我好兴奋！” 小美人鱼大喊，“我等这一天等了好久了。” 
At last it was the Little Mermaid’s fifteenth birthday.
"Today you will finally be able to see the Upper World," said her grandmother.
"I’m so excited!" squealed the Little Mermaid. "I’ve been waiting so long for this day."
智慧人鱼把一个白百合编成的花环，戴在小美人鱼的头上。然后，在她的尾巴上挂上了象征着王族的四个牡蛎。
“哎呀，好疼！” 小美人鱼大喊。
The Wise Mermaid placed a wreath of white lilies on the Little Mermaid’s head. Then she hooked four oysters, a sign of royalty, on the Little Mermaid's tail.
"Ouch! That hurts!" cried the Little Mermaid.
“这是成为十五岁的一个小牺牲。” 奶奶说。
小美人鱼也承认牡蛎看起来很美。她渴望到地上世界的冒险。
"It's a small sacrifice for becoming a 15-year-old princess," said her grandmother.
The Little Mermaid agreed the oysters looked lovely. She was anxious to start her adventure in the Upper World.
“快点儿，奶奶。” 小美人鱼恳求说。
“快去吧，宝贝。” 奶奶说，“但别呆太久了。记住，你的家在这里，在海底。” 
小美人鱼急忙着拥抱了奶奶，说了声再见，就游走了。 
"Please hurry, Grandmother," she begged.
"Go ahead, dear," her grandmother said. "But don’t stay too long. Remember, your home is here under the sea."
The Little Mermaid hurriedly hugged her grandmother good-bye and swam away.
当小美人鱼到达海面时，太阳刚落山。她看见天空从蓝色变成了粉色。到了夜晚，天空变成了深紫色，星光闪闪。很快，月亮光照到了海面上。
When the Little Mermaid got to the surface, the sun was just setting. She saw the sky turn from blue to pink. As it became night, the sky turned deep purple and stars sparkled. Soon moonlight shone on the sea.
“地上世界好美啊！” 小美人鱼大喊。
远远地，小美人鱼看见一艘船。她游近了一点，看见船停在水里一动不动。音乐响着，她看见了明亮、彩色的光。船员们在船的甲板上走来走去。他们用两条腿走路。
"The Upper World is so beautiful!" exclaimed the Little Mermaid.
In the distance, she saw a ship. She swam closer and saw that the ship was standing still in the water. Music was playing, and she saw bright, colorful lights. Sailors moved about on the ship’s deck. They walked around on two legs.
“是人类？” 她说，“好奇怪。” 
她兴奋地一直游到船边。从外向里面看，里面有更多的人在唱歌、跳舞。所有人都穿着漂亮的衣服。
"Humans?" she said. "How wonderfully strange."
The Little Mermaid swam excitedly right up to the side of the ship and was on outside looking in. Inside, more humans were singing and dancing. All of them were dressed in fine clothes.
“看起来是个派对！” 她说。
小美人鱼仔细地看着每个人。最后，她注意到了一个年轻人。“哦，他好帅。” 她想。当所有人都往甲板上走时，她目不转睛地看着他。
"It looks like a party!" she said.
The Little Mermaid looked at each human carefully. Then she saw a young man. "Oh, how very handsome," she thought. When everyone went up on the desk, the Little Mermaid kept watching him.
“我们记得十六年前您出生时的样子，王子。” 一个头上戴着蓝色羽毛的女人说。
“他是个王子！” 小美人鱼说。
另一个客人说：“你有善良的心。以后，您会成为一位伟大的国王！” 
"We remember when you were born, dear Prince, 16 years ago," said a woman with a blue feather in her hair.
"He’s a prince!" exclaimed the Little Mermaid.
Another guest said, "With your kind heart, you’ll be a great king someday!"
“祝您每年都像今年这么幸福！” 一个男人举杯说。
“生日快乐，王子！” 人群一起欢呼。
“每个人都爱他。” 小美人鱼想，“他一定很棒。” 
"May every year be as happy as this one!" added a man, holding up his glass.
"Happy Birthday, Prince!" the crowd cheered.
"Everyone loves him," thought the Little Mermaid. "He must be wonderful."
烟花冲上天空，化作数千个小火花。水面反射出火花，整个大海闪闪发光。
“哦，天啊！” 小美人鱼从来没见过这样的情景。她吓得飞快地游到了水底。
Rockets shot high into the sky and turned into a thousand tiny sparks. The water reflected the sparks, and the entire ocean glowed.
"Oh my!" The mermaid had never seen such a sight. Frightened, she quickly swam underwater.
但是，她从水底看上去，很快就不再害怕了。“天上有好多闪闪发光的火花！” 她兴奋地说。
王子跟客人拥抱、握手，说：“谢谢你们为我庆祝。” 
But looking up through the water, she soon stopped being afraid. "There are so many sparkling flowers in the sky!" she said excitedly.
The Prince hugged his guests, shook their hands, and said, "Thank you for the celebration."
小美人鱼游到了海面上看。王子是所有人类中最帅的。他跟她花园的雕像一样帅。
但是，很快，音乐就停下了。五颜六色的灯也灭了。
The Little Mermaid swam up to the surface and watched. The Prince was the most beautiful of all the humans. He was as beautiful as the statue in her garden.
But soon the music stopped. The colorful lights were put out.
“谢谢你们帮忙！” 王子对他的船员们说，“这个派对太棒了。” 
然后，王子和他的客人们走到甲板下去睡觉。可是，小美人鱼不想离开船，还有英俊的王子。因为她对王子一见钟情了！
"Thanks for all your help!" said the Prince to his crew. "The party was great."
Then the Prince and his guests went below the deck to sleep. But the Little Mermaid did not want to leave the ship and the handsome Prince. It was because she had fallen in love with the Prince at first sight!
突然，天上出现了黑色的乌云。天上下起了暴雨。波涛汹涌，就像是高高的黑山。
“哦，真好玩儿！” 小美人鱼想，“暴风雨来了！” 
Suddenly black clouds appeared in the sky. Rain poured down heavily. The waves were rough like tall black mountains.
"Oh, what fun!" thought the Little Mermaid. "A storm is coming!"
在海底王国，即使暴风雨来了，她和她的姐姐们也很安全。她们喜欢在汹涌的海水里玩儿。
可是，船员们很害怕。他们拉住绳子。王子的船开始摇动。
In the Sea Kingdom, she and her sisters were safe from storms. They loved to play in the churning water.
But the sailors were frightened. They pulled on ropes. The Prince’s ship began to rock.
船在暴雨中摇晃。船摇晃得越来越厉害。
“所有船员们都到甲板上来！” 一个船员大喊。
“船要沉了！” 另一个船员大喊。
小美人鱼不害怕，她在远处等着。
Storms rocked the ship around. The ship rocked more and more severely.
"All sailors to the deck!" shouted one sailor.
"The ship seems like it will sink!" shouted another.
Unafraid, the Little Mermaid waited in the distance.
在黑暗中，她看着船想：“我真幸运！现在王子不能离开了！也许早上前我能再见他一面。” 小美人鱼并不知道船陷入了危险。
Through the darkness, she watched the ship and thought, "Lucky me! Now the Prince can’t go away! Maybe I can see him again before morning." The Little Mermaid didn’t know that the ship was in danger.
暴风雨越来越厉害。巨大的波涛拍打着甲板。风吹着船帆，把船帆撕开了。然后，主桅杆突然破裂掉进了水里。船一倾斜，海水就灌进了甲板。
The storm grew worse and worse. Huge waves crashed on the deck. The wind blew against the sails and tore them apart. Then, without warning, the main mast snapped and fell into the water. The ship tilted to tip to one side, and seawater flowed onto the deck.
“放下救生艇！” 一个船员大喊，“我们必须救乘客！” 可是，已经太晚了。一道闪电击中船，一股狂风吹灭了所有的灯。突然，四周黑了下来。
"Get out the lifeboats!" cried a sailor. "We must save the passengers!" But it was already too late. Lightning struck the ship, and a strong gust of wind blew out all the lights. Suddenly everything went pitch-black.
然后，又一道闪电击中，船起火了。船开始燃烧起来。
在闪电下，小美人鱼看见人类在求生。
Then lightning struck again and set the ship on fire. The ship began to burn.
In the fire, the Little Mermaid could see that the humans were struggling to stay alive.
“哦，不。” 她担心地说，“可怜的人类！” 
一些人掉进了海里。另一些人从着火的甲板上往下跳。
“救命！救命！” 人们尖叫着掉进水里。
"Oh no," she cried, "the poor humans!"
Some of them fell into the sea. Others jumped from the burning decks.
"Help! Help!" they screamed as they fell into the water.
小美人鱼看见那个戴着蓝色羽毛的女人抱着一个桌子腿。一个男人拼命向海边游去。
“救命！” 一个客人大喊。
“我不想死！” 另一个客人大喊。所有人都拼命让头浮在水面。
The Little Mermaid saw the woman who wore the blue feather holding onto a table leg. One man was trying to swim to shore.
"Help!" cried one guest.
"I don’t want to die!" cried another. All struggled to keep their heads above the surface.
小美人鱼拼命地找王子。终于，她看见了王子。他在努力挣扎，可是他太累了。他开始往下沉。
The Little Mermaid searched desperately for the Prince. Finally, she saw him. He was struggling, but he was becoming exhausted. He began to sink down.
“他要去访问海底王国！” 小美人鱼开心地说。可是，她记起来人类不能在海底生活。人类和人鱼不一样，人类不能在水里呼吸。
“我的王子会死的！哦，我必须去救他！” 她担心地说。
"He’s going to visit the Sea Kingdom!" said the Little Mermaid happily. But she remembered that humans could not live beneath the sea. Humans were different from merpeople, and they could not breathe underwater.
"My Prince will die! Oh, I must save him!" she cried.
小美人鱼向破碎的船游过去。她拼命地游。最后，她发现了快沉下去的王子。她游到了王子身边。
The Little Mermaid swam toward the destroyed ship. She swam desperately. At last, she discovered the sinking Prince. She swam close to him.
他看起来像一片从树上掉下来的叶子。小美人鱼抓住他，把他的头托到了水上。
“加油，亲爱的王子，你安全了！” 她在他的耳边小声地说。然后，她顺着波涛游到了海边。
He looked like a leaf falling from a tree. The Little Mermaid grabbed him and pulled his head above the water.
"Hang on, dear Prince, you're safe now!" she whispered in his ear. Then she let the waves take her and the Prince toward the shore.
日出时分，暴风雨停了。小美人鱼拉着王子游向海边。然后，她把王子放到了白色的沙滩上。
“你现在安全了。” 她跟王子说。可是，王子没醒过来，听不见她说的话。
By sunrise, the storm had stopped. The Little Mermaid swam to the shore, pulling the Prince through the water. Then she laid the Prince on the white sand.
"You’re safe now," she told him. But he was not awake and could not hear what she had said.
小美人鱼看着四周。她在海滩上，旁边有一个小花园。花园里有白色长椅，周围环绕着柠檬和橙子树。她看见花园后有一个高大的房子。
The Little Mermaid looked around. She was in a little cove, and there was a small garden just beyond the beach. The garden had white benches surrounded by sweet-smelling lemon and orange trees. Just behind the garden, she saw a large building.
“好高的塔，好大的门啊！” 小美人鱼说。
她扶着王子的头，手指拨开他湿湿的头发。“你醒醒吧，王子。” 小美人鱼说。可是，王子还是没醒过来。
"There's a tall tower and a big door!!" cried the Little Mermaid.
She held the Prince’s head and ran her fingers through his wet hair. "Please wake up, my Prince," said the Little Mermaid. But he did not wake up.
“你睡觉的时候，阳光会温暖你的。” 她深情地说。小美人鱼看着他英俊的脸，亲了亲他的眼皮。“他甚至比我花园里的那个雕像还要帅。” 她想，“不过，我的王子是真实的。” 
"As you sleep, the sun will warm you," she lovingly told the Prince. The Little Mermaid stared at his handsome face and then kissed his eyelids. "He looks even more handsome than the statue in my garden," she thought. "But my Prince is real."
突然，小美人鱼听见一个声音。塔发出巨大的声音。小美人鱼抬头看。“那是教堂的铃声吗？” 
一群少女从大门里走出来。
Suddenly the Little Mermaid heard a sound. There was a loud noise from the tower. The Little Mermaid looked up. "Could that sound be church bells?"
A group of young girls came out of the large doors.
她们穿着简洁的白裙，头发用白色的蝴蝶结扎了起来。
“她们是谁？在这里做什么？” 小美人鱼很好奇。
少女们围坐在长椅上，有说有笑。
They were dressed in simple, white dresses, and their hair was tied with white ribbons.
"Who are they? What are they doing here?" wondered the mermaid.
The girls gathered around the benches and sat, laughing and talking.
“不能让人类看见我！” 小美人鱼害怕地说，“请多保重，我的王子。” 小美人鱼离开了海滩，游向一块岩石。小美人鱼为了不让人类发现，躲在海水泡沫里。然后，她看着海滩。
"The humans must not see me!" said the Little Mermaid, fearfully. "Please stay safe, my Prince." The mermaid left the beach and swam to a rock. She hid herself in sea foam so no one would see her. Then she watched the beach.
“看！” 一个少女对朋友们喊。虽然她穿着和别的少女一样的衣服，但她更漂亮。她有一双绿色的大眼睛，睫毛又长又黑。她跑向海滩，看着那个年轻人。王子还是一动不动。“过来！我发现了一个人！” 
"Look!" shouted one girl to her friends. Although she was dressed like the others, she was more beautiful. She had big, green eyes and long, dark lashes. She ran to the beach and stared at the young man. The Prince still had not moved. "Come here! I’ve found someone!"
少女碰了碰他的脸，确认他还活着。就在这时，他醒了，对着她笑。
“哦，梅丹，你发现了什么？” 一个少女咯咯笑着问。然后，她们都跑向海滩，围在王子身边。
The girl touched his face to make sure he was real. And at that moment, he woke up and smiled at her.
"Oh, Maiden, what have you found?" called one giggling girl. Then they all ran down to the beach and gathered around the Prince.
当王子看见围着他的美丽少女们时，他笑得更开心了。可是，他一直看着那个发现他的，被叫做梅丹的少女。“谢谢你救了我。” 他说。
When the Prince saw all the pretty, young girls around him, he smiled happily. But he kept looking at the girl who had found him, who was called Maiden. "Thank you for saving me," he said.
“他应该谢谢我！” 小美人鱼想，“是我救了他的命，他却不知道。” 
“去教堂暖暖吧。” 梅丹说。她领着他走过去。
"He should be thanking me!" thought the Little Mermaid. "I saved his life, and he doesn’t even know it."
"Come inside to get warm," said Maiden. She led the Prince.
别的少女跟在后面，走进了大门。
“她们从我身边带走了我的王子。” 小美人鱼在岩石后说。如果她会哭，泪水会一滴一滴地落进大海。
The others followed and then went through the big doors.
"They’re taking my Prince away from me," the Little Mermaid said from behind her rock. If she could have cried, her tears would have fallen one by one into the sea.
可是，人鱼不会哭，所以没有眼泪掉进汹涌的波涛里。
“我要回到海底王国的家里去。” 小美人鱼说，“但是，我会因为你回来的，我的王子。” 说完，她跳进了海里。
But merpeople are unable to cry, so no tears fell into the crashing waves.
"I must go home to the Sea Kingdom," said the Little Mermaid. "But I’ll come back for you, my Prince." After speaking, she dived underwater.
刚开始，小美人鱼决定把她对王子的爱保密。“我不会告诉任何人。” 她想，“如果我不提起他，也许我会忘记他。” 可是，她没法想别的事情。
At first, the Little Mermaid decided to keep her love for the Prince a secret. "I must not tell anyone," she thought. "Maybe if I don't speak of him, I might forget about him." But she could think of nothing else.
最后，她告诉了大姐。
“我爱上了一个来自地上世界的人。” 
“哦，不！” 大姐大喊，“谁？” 
“他是王子。” 小美人鱼说，“可是，我不知道他的王国在哪儿或者他住在哪儿。” 
Finally, she told her eldest sister, "I have fallen in love with an Upper World person."
"Oh no!" cried her sister. "Who?"
"He is a prince," said the Little Mermaid. "But I don’t know where his kingdom is or where he lives."
大姐把小美人鱼说的话告诉了其他人鱼。很快，一个人鱼就听说了这件事，来看爱上王子的小美人鱼。
“我知道他住在哪儿。” 她告诉公主，“他的王宫在一座山上，就在海边山崖上。” 
Her sister told other merpeople what the Little Mermaid had said. Soon one mermaid heard the tale and visited the princess, who had fallen in love with the Prince.
"I know where he lives," the mermaid said. "His palace is on a mountain, just beyond the sea cliffs."
“请带我去那里。” 小美人鱼求她。
“我们没有腿，不能走到他家门口。” 那个人鱼说，“可是，我们可以游到山崖边去。从那高高的岩石上，我们能看见一个小阳台。王子有时候会站在那里。” 
"Please take me there," begged the Little Mermaid.
"We have no legs and cannot walk to his door," the other mermaid said. "But we can swim to the cliffs. From the high rocks, we can see the little balcony where the Prince sometimes stands."
在那个人鱼的带领下，小美人鱼游到了山崖边，爬上了附近的岩石。从那里，她看见一座用金色岩石建成的王宫。一个楼梯从大海延伸到王宫门口。王宫一侧，的确有一个阳台。
With her guide, the Little Mermaid swam to the cliffs and climbed onto the nearby rocks. From there she saw a palace built of golden stones. A steep stairway led from the ocean to the palace entrance. And sure enough, on one side of the palace was a balcony.
“哦，也许我能看见他！” 小美人鱼在一块岩石后远望着说。她一直看着王宫，带她来的人鱼就游走了。
从那天起，小美人鱼就每天晚上到山崖边去。
"Oh, maybe I will see him!" cried the Little Mermaid, peering from behind a rock. She kept watching the palace, and the mermaid who had come with the princess swam away.
From that day on, the Little Mermaid went to the cliffs every evening.
她坐在岩石上等着那个英俊的王子。有时候，她能看见王子一个人站在明亮的月光下。
“哦，王子！我好想跟你说话。” 小美人鱼说，“可是，我够不到你。所以，我要给你唱歌。” 
She sat on a rock and waited for her handsome Prince. Once in a while, she could see him standing alone in the bright moonlight.
"Oh, Prince, I want to talk with you," she said. "But I can’t reach you. So I’ll sing to you instead."
小美人鱼认为王子如果听到她的声音，就能认出她。在海底王国，每个人都说她的嗓子是所有人鱼中最甜美的。
The Little Mermaid thought the Prince would notice her if he heard her voice. In the Sea Kingdom, everyone said she had the sweetest voice of all the merpeople.
我住在大海里。
你住在地上，我无法靠近。
你英俊的脸在我心里。
我的心在你手里。
找我吧，王子，找我吧！
My home is in the sea.
Your home is on land, a place I cannot go.
Your handsome face is in my heart.
My whole heart is in your hands.
Come to me. My Prince, come to me!
可是，他在陆地上，她在海里。在星光下，海风带走了她的声音。太远了，无法被听到。所以，她只能远望着王子。
But he was on land, and she was in the sea. Under the starlight, the ocean breeze carried her voice away. It was carried away too far away to be heard. So she could only watch the Prince from afar.
有几个晚上，小美人鱼会游到港口，希望能见到他。可是，大多时候，她只能看见渔民们拿着渔网，有说有笑。
“王子又聪明，又年轻。” 一个渔民说，“总有一天，他会成为一个伟大的国王。” 
Some nights the Little Mermaid would swim to the harbor, hoping to see him on the dock. But most of the time, she saw only fishermen with their nets, talking and laughing.
"The Prince is smart and young," a fisherman said. "One day he will be a great king."
小美人鱼想起王子躺在沙滩时自己和王子在一起的时光。
“想想啊，是我救了王子的命！” 她颤抖着，充满了自豪和爱。
The Little Mermaid thought about her time with the Prince as he lay on the shore.
"And think—it was I who saved his life!" She shivered with pride and love.
小美人鱼想起她亲了王子。
可是，王子不知道真相。
“我希望有一天他能知道真相。” 她渴望着。
The Little Mermaid remembered how she had kissed the Prince.
But the Prince did not know the truth.
"I hope he’ll come to know the truth someday," she desperately hoped.
每次到地上世界，小美人鱼都觉得不开心。
“王子的世界比我的要大得多。” 小美人鱼自言自语，“人类能在陆地上走，能航海。他们的生活特别有意思，我的生活太无聊了。” 
Every time the Little Mermaid visited the Upper World, she felt unhappy.
"The Prince’s world is so much bigger than mine," said the mermaid to herself. "Humans can walk on land and sail the seas. Their lives are so interesting, and my life is so boring."
小美人鱼觉得自己更想生活在地上世界，而不是海底王国。一天又一天，她一个人坐在自己的花园里。姐姐们很担心，于是她们去找奶奶。
The Little Mermaid thought about how she’d wanted to live in the Upper World more than the Sea Kingdom. Day after day she sat alone in her garden. Her sisters became worried and went to their grandmother.
“奶奶。” 大姐说，“请跟小美人鱼谈谈，弄清楚她为什么那么忧伤。” 
于是，智慧人鱼去看她的孙女，把她抱在怀里。“宝贝，告诉我你为什么忧伤。” 
"Grandmother," said the eldest sister, "please talk to the Little Mermaid, and find out why she’s so gloomy."
So the Wise Mermaid went to her grandchild and took the Little Mermaid in her arms. "Sweet one, tell me what is bothering you."
“奶奶。” 小美人鱼说，“有永生不死的人吗？” 
“我告诉过你。” 智慧人鱼回答，“人类比我们死得早，但是他们的灵魂会永生。” 
"Grandmother," said the Little Mermaid, "Are there humans that live forever?"
"I told you," the Wise Mermaid answered. "They die much sooner than we do, but their souls live forever."
“我也想拥有灵魂。我想在地上世界生活。” 
“你的家在这里。” 智慧人鱼说，“相信我，世界上没有地方比得上海底王国。我们在这里都很幸福。这里是我们的家。” 
"I want a soul too. And I want to live in the Upper World."
"Your place is here," said the Wise Mermaid. "Believe me, there is no better place in the world than the Sea Kingdom. We are all very happy here. This is our home."
“可是，我想拥有和我的王子一样的灵魂。那样我们俩就能永远在一起了。” 小美人鱼坚持说。
“你不需要灵魂。” 智慧人鱼坚定说。
"But I want a soul like my Prince. Then we could live forever together," the Little Mermaid said stubbornly.
"You don’t need a soul," replied the Wise Mermaid, firmly.
“作为一个人鱼，你会活很长时间。当你死的时候，你会变成美丽的大海泡沫。这样，你就能一直是大海的一部分和我们的一部分。” 
"As a mermaid, you will live for a very long time. And when you die, you will become beautiful sea foam. This way you will remain part of the sea and a part of all of us."
小美人鱼恳求说：“求您了，奶奶，告诉我怎么才能得到灵魂。” 
智慧人鱼意识到她无法改变她孙女的想法。
The Little Mermaid pleaded, "Please, Grandmother, tell me how I can get a soul."
The Wise Mermaid realized that she could not change her granddaughter’s mind.
“嗯，我听说人鱼一定要和一个人类结婚。” 她说，“你的王子必须要爱上你，并且和你结婚，你才能变成拥有灵魂的人类。” 
"Well, I’ve heard that merpeople must marry humans," she said. "Your Prince must fall in love with you, then marry you; then you could become a human and have a soul."
“所以，我要做的就是和王子结婚？” 小美人鱼充满希望地问。
“我不确定。” 智慧人鱼说，“我告诉你的只是一个古老的传说。我没听说过有人鱼得到灵魂或想拥有灵魂。我们没有灵魂，也生活得很幸福。” 
"So all I have to do is marry the Prince?" asked the Little Mermaid hopefully.
"I do not know for sure," replied the Wise Mermaid. "What I have told you is only an old tale. I know of no mermaid who has ever received a soul—or even wanted one. We don't have souls, and we live very happily."
可是，小美人鱼听不进去奶奶的话。她已经在忙着思考和制定计划了。
“奶奶，为了在地上世界生活，我必须做什么？” 她问。
But the Little Mermaid was not listening to her grandmother’s words. She was already too busy thinking and making plans.
"Grandmother, what must I do to live in the Upper World?" she asked.
“不要再想了。” 智慧人鱼说，“要想生活在那里，你得有腿和脚。” 她摸了摸孙女的头，“因为你有美丽的尾巴，你不可能在陆地上生活。” 
"Don't think about it," said the Wise Mermaid. "To live there you would need legs and feet." She patted her granddaughter on the head. "Because of your beautiful tail, living on land is impossible."
小美人鱼不开心地看着自己的尾巴，叹了口气。“我讨厌我的尾巴。” 
智慧人鱼摇头说：“你很漂亮。现在去玩儿吧。别想地上世界了。” 
The Little Mermaid looked unhappily at her body and sighed. "I hate my tail."
The Wise Mermaid shook her head and said, "You are lovely. Now go and play. Stop thinking about the Upper World."
小美人鱼游走了，但是她没玩儿。她想：“一定有办法的。一定有人能帮我。也许海巫婆能帮助我。” 
The Little Mermaid swam away, but she didn’t play. She thought, 'There must be some way. Someone must be able to help me. Maybe the Sea Witch can help me.'
海巫婆住在一个黑暗地方的边上，那个地方叫固巴。据说，那里的漩涡能吞噬所有的生物。大部分的人鱼不敢游到那附近。他们听说过可怕的故事，说好奇的人鱼游近固巴，永远也没能再回到家里。
The Sea Witch lived at the edge of a dark place called Gooba. It was said that its whirlpools could devour all living things. Most merpeople were afraid to swim there. They’d heard scary stories about curious merpeople who swam too close to Gooba and never returned home.
“我小时候，奶奶告诉过我海巫婆的家。” 小美人鱼边想边游，“但应该不是那么可怕的。” 
"When I was little, Grandmother told me about the home of the Sea Witch," the Little Mermaid thought as she swam. "But it can’t be that awful."
“停下，公主。” 一条小鱼突然大喊，“别去那个可怕的地方。” 
“别担心，小鱼。” 小美人鱼说。然后，她心里想：“海巫婆能把我变成人类。她一定能的。” 
"Stop, Princess," a small fish suddenly cried. "Do not go to that terrible place."
"Don’t worry, little fish," said the Little Mermaid. Then she thought to herself, "The Sea Witch can turn me into a human. I just know she can."
爱上了王子的小美人鱼继续游，进入了海巫婆的灰色地带。“海底王国很美，这里却真可怕。” 小美人鱼自言自语。没有闪光的白色沙子，这里又暗又粘。到处是淤泥。
The Little Mermaid, who had fallen in love with the Prince, swam on and entered the gray territory of the Sea Witch. "The Sea Kingdom is beautiful, but this place is terrible," she said to herself. There was no shiny white sand, and it was dark and slimy. It was muddy everywhere.
“海花在哪儿？” 小美人鱼觉得很奇怪，“海草在哪儿？” 
“从固巴出去，公主。” 一条蛇从一个洞口嘶嘶地说。
"Where are the sea flowers?" the Little Mermaid thought strangely. "Where is the seaweed?"
"Get out of Gooba, Princess," hissed a snake from the mouth of a cave.
“是谁？” 小美人鱼害怕地说。
蛇消失在黑暗的洞里。
突然，小美人鱼看见转来转去的漩涡。
"Who’s that?" asked the Little Mermaid, frightened.
The snake disappeared into the dark cave.
Suddenly, the Little Mermaid saw whirlpools twirling around.
漩涡吞噬了近处的一切。所有东西都被吸进了淤泥里。
The whirlpools swallowed up everything nearby. Everything got stuck in the mud.
“我不害怕。” 她对自己说，“我的尾巴很健壮，能让我很容易地避开那种危险。” 她游过了一个又一个的漩涡。
然后，小美人鱼进入了一片泥泞的森林，树上都是干巴巴的树枝。
"I am not afraid," the Little Mermaid said to herself. "My tail is strong and I can get away from danger easily." And she swam around the whirlpools in her path one by one.
Next, the Little Mermaid entered a muddy forest where the tree had bare branches.
每根长树枝上都有小树枝，像手指一样动，抓住附近的一切。树枝上粘着鱼骨头和沉船的碎片。船员们的骷髅挂在树枝上。
Each long branch had twigs that moved like fingers to grab anything nearby. Fish bones and pieces of wood from sunken ships. Sailors' skulls too hung from the tree branches.
“啊，天啊。” 小美人鱼想，“那些树绝对不会放开抓住的东西！” 小美人鱼吓得停了下来。“我想我做不到！” 她害怕地说。她的心脏吓得直跳，差点就回头了。可是，她想起了她的王子。“为了我的爱，” 她想，“我得继续前进。” 
"Oh dear," thought the Little Mermaid, "the trees never let go of what they catch!" Terrified, she stopped. "I don’t think I can do this!" she said in fear. Her little heart beat with fear and she almost turned back. But then she remembered her Prince. "For my love," she thought, "I must keep going."
在进入森林之前，小美人鱼把头发扎了起来。然后，她像鱼一样飞快地游了过去。树枝伸出手指要抓她。可是，她太快了。树枝没能抓到她。
Before she entered the forest, the Little Mermaid tied her hair. Then she swam quickly like a nimble fish. The branches stretched out their fingers to grab her. But she was too fast. The branches could not catch her.
最后，她来到大海森林里的空地。胖胖的虫子们站着，长长的蛇四处爬。小美人鱼看见一个用骨头盖的房子。海巫婆坐在前面，用一个贝壳剔着牙。她头发乱蓬蓬的，眼睛像珠子，看起来很吓人。她的脚边有几只青蛙在呱呱地向她叫。
Finally she arrived at a clearing in the sea forest. Fat worms were standing, and long snakes slithered about. The Little Mermaid saw a house made of bones. The Sea Witch was sitting in front, picking her teeth with a seashell. With her tangled hair and beady eyes, she looked horrifying. At her feet sat frogs that croaked at her.
“欢迎光临，小美人鱼。我在等你呢。” 海巫婆说，“我知道你为什么来这儿。你想变成人类。真的太傻了！” 说完，海巫婆大笑起来。
"Welcome, Little Mermaid. I've been waiting for you," said the Sea Witch. "And I know why you are here. You want to be a human. What a very foolish idea!" Then the Sea Witch began to laugh.
海巫婆仔细地看了看小美人鱼。“我要为你准备一个魔法药。” 她说，“它能把你的尾巴变成腿和脚。” 
“太好了！” 小美人鱼开心地说，“有了腿，我就能走到陆地上，走近我的王子了。那么，他就一定会爱上我！” 
The Sea Witch watched the mermaid closely. "I will prepare a magic potion for you," she said, "It will change your tail into legs and feet."
"Wonderful!" said the Little Mermaid happily. "With legs, I can walk on land and go near my Prince. Then surely he will fall in love with me!"
“我得警告你。” 海巫婆接着说，“每当你站着的时候，都会很痛。在别人看来，你走起路来很优美。可是，你每走一步，都会像一个锋利的刀割你的脚一样痛。” 
"I must warn you," continued the Sea Witch. "Every time you stand, it will hurt very much. Your walk will look beautiful to other people. But to you, every step you take will hurt like a sharp knife cutting into your feet."
海巫婆停下来抚摸她的青蛙。然后，她问小美人鱼：“你愿意为你所愿而受苦吗？” 
“我当然愿意！” 小美人鱼兴奋地回答，“为了活在地上世界并得到灵魂，我愿意做任何事情。” 
The Sea Witch paused to pet one of her frogs. Then she asked the Little Mermaid, "Are you willing to suffer to get what you want?"
"Yes, of course!" replied the Little Mermaid excitedly. "In order to live in the Upper World and get a soul, I will do anything."
“你要记住，” 海巫婆说，“一旦你变成了人，你就永远不可能再变回人鱼。你永远不能再住在你父亲的王宫里或者和你的姐姐们玩儿。你不能再问奶奶问题。你不能再回到海底王国。” 
"Remember," said the witch, "once you become human, you can never be a mermaid again. You will no longer be able to live in your father’s palace or play with your sisters. You can no longer ask your grandmother questions. You can never come back to the Sea Kingdom again."
“我明白。” 小美人鱼轻轻地说。
海巫婆盯着小美人鱼的眼睛。
“还有一件事，宝贝。” 她接着说，
"I understand," said the Little Mermaid softly.
The Sea Witch looked right into the mermaid’s eyes.
"One more thing, my dear," she added.
“如果王子跟别人结婚，你就不能得到灵魂。在他跟别人结婚的第二天早上，你就会死掉变成大海泡沫。” 
“我愿意冒险。” 小美人鱼说，脸色变得苍白。“我知道我要让王子爱上我。” 
"If the Prince doesn’t marry you, you cannot get a soul. And the morning after he marries another, you will die and become sea foam."
"I will take that risk," said the Little Mermaid, turning pale. "I know I can make the Prince fall in love with me."
“嗯，孩子，为了确定我们的交易，你必须得付出代价。” 海巫婆说，“你拥有所有人鱼中最美的声音。大家都说你的声音很特别。如果你把你的声音给我，我就给你药。” 
"Well, child, to seal our deal, you must pay the price," said the witch. "You have the sweetest voice of all the merpeople. Everyone says it is very special. Give me your voice, and I will give you the potion."
“可是，那我怎么告诉王子我爱他呢？” 小美人鱼说。
“那么，你现在失去勇气了吗？” 海巫婆笑着问。
“不。” 小美人鱼回答。她想起自己为家人们唱歌的时候。
"But how will I tell the Prince I love him?" said the Little Mermaid.
"So have you lost your courage now?" asked the Sea Witch, laughing.
"No," the Little Mermaid answered. She thought of the times she sang for her family.
想起那些幸福的时刻，小美人鱼觉得很伤心。她慢慢地说：“我要把我的声音给你。” 
“那跟我来吧。” 海巫婆带着小美人鱼走进了她的房子。
Remembering those happy moments, the Little Mermaid felt very sad. Slowly she whispered, "I will give you my voice."
"Then follow me." The Sea Witch took the Little Mermaid to her house.
一进到里面，海巫婆就把一口大锅放到了地上冒着烟的坑上。海巫婆切了几条蛇和几只水母，把它们扔进了大锅，还扔进去别的一些黏糊糊的东西。然后，海巫婆开始念咒语。
Once inside, the witch placed a large pot over a hot, steaming hole in the floor. She chopped up some snakes and jellyfish and threw the pieces into the pot with some other slimy things. Then the Sea Witch began to chant a spell:
“小美人鱼请求，爱一个只活在地上世界的王子。
现在，她已经下了决心，她将拥有腿，我将拥有她的声音。” 
What The Little Mermaid wants,
Is to love a prince who can only live in the Upper World. Now she has decided,
That she’ll have legs, I’ll have her voice!
液体开始沸腾。 鬼脸一样的烟雾从大锅里升起来。魔法药做好了。海巫婆把它倒进了一个小瓶子。
The liquid began to boil. Smoke like ghostly faces rose from the pot. The magic potion was ready. The sea witch poured it into a small bottle.
“这就是你要的。” 海巫婆说。她把瓶子递给小美人鱼。
小美人鱼想说点什么，可是她发不出声音。她美丽的声音消失了。
"This is what you came for," said the Sea Witch. She handed the bottle to the Little Mermaid.
The mermaid wanted to say something, but no sound came out. Her beautiful voice had disappeared.
“在日出之前，游到海边上去。” 海巫婆命令说，“等你到了干的陆地，把药喝了。你的尾巴就会变成一双腿脚。现在，去吧！” 
小美人鱼默默地点了点头。
"Before sunrise, swim to shore," the Sea Witch commanded. "When you are on dry land, drink the potion I gave you. Your tail will become a pair of legs and feet. Now go."
The Little Mermaid nodded silently.
在离开以前，小美人鱼想见见她的家人们。但是，当她到王宫时，大家都睡着了。她感到巨大的悲伤。“我要永远离开我的家了。” 她想，“我连再见也说不了。” 然后，在日出之前，她向王子王宫附近的山崖游去。
Before leaving, the Little Mermaid wanted to see her family. But when she reached her father’s palace, everyone was asleep. She felt a great sadness. "I’m leaving my home forever," she thought. "And I can’t even say good-bye." Then, just before sunrise, she swam to the cliffs near the Prince’s palace.
爬上岩石后，小美人鱼能看见那个阳台。一爬到海边上，她就喝了药。突然，她感到剧痛，剧痛使她失去了意识。那时，她看见自己的尾巴开始裂成两半。
After she climbed on the rocks, the Little Mermaid could see the balcony. Once on shore, she drank the potion. Suddenly she felt a horrible pain and she fainted. As she fainted, the Little Mermaid saw her tail begin to split in half.
当她醒过来，她发现自己躺在沙发上。她的身上盖着一个斗篷。她抬头看去，看见王子站在她身边。
When she awoke, she found herself on a sofa. Her body was covered by a cape. She looked up and saw the Prince standing over her.
“我看见你躺在岩石上。” 王子说，“你是谁？你从哪儿来？” 
“我是你的真爱。” 她想说，“我在海里救了你的命。” 可是，她说不出话来。所以，她对王子笑，希望他能明白。
"I saw you lying on the rocks," the Prince said. "Who are you and where are you come from?"
"I am your true love," she wanted to say. "I saved you from the sea." But she could not speak. So she smiled at the Prince, hoping he would understand.
“你说吧，别害怕。” 王子温柔地说。
可是，小美人鱼只能看着他。
“你叫什么名字？” 他问。
小美人鱼说不出话来。
"Please speak; Don’t be afraid," the Prince said warmly.
But the Little Mermaid could only look at him.
"What is your name?" he asked.
The Little Mermaid could not speak.
最后，他说：“我不知道你为什么不说话，但你还是需要我的帮助。” 王子帮小美人鱼站了起来。就和海巫婆说的一样，小美人鱼每走一步，都非常痛。
Finally he said, "I do not know why you will not speak. But you still need my help." The Prince helped the Little Mermaid stand up. As the Sea Witch had said, every step the Little Mermaid took felt very painful.
但在王子眼里，她看起来很完美。他们穿过大厅时，小美人鱼像云彩一样轻快地走着。
“你真优雅。” 王子边说边带着她走进了自己的房间。
But in the eyes of the Prince, she looked perfect. As they walked through the hall, she walked as lightly as a cloud.
"How graceful you are," the Prince said as he led her into his rooms.
他让他的仆人拿来一些衣服。“把她打扮成王室的贵宾！” 他命令说。然后，他转向她温柔地问：“今天你愿意陪我参加王室的音乐会吗？” 
He asked his servants to bring them some clothes. "Dress her as a guest of the royal palace!" he commanded. Then he turned to her and asked softly, "Will you accompany me to the royal performance today?"
小美人鱼期待地点了点头。仆人给她穿上了一条连衣裙后，王子带着她来到王宫大厅，把她介绍给了父母。
“你真美！” 王后感叹道说，“你从哪儿来？” 可是，小美人鱼不能回答。
The Little Mermaid nodded in anticipation. After the servants dressed her in a dress, the Prince took her to the palace hall to introduce her to his parents.
"How beautiful you are!" exclaimed the Queen. "Where are you come from?" But of course, the Little Mermaid could not answer.
一个高个子女人走进大厅为王子和他的父母表演。她唱得非常甜美，王子高兴地鼓掌。小美人鱼伤心地想：“我曾经唱得更甜美。” 
A tall woman entered the hall to perform for the Prince and his parents. She sang very sweetly, and the Prince clapped his hands in delight. The Little Mermaid sadly thought, "I used to sing even more sweetly."
可是，小美人鱼听着听着，她惊讶地发现自己想跳舞。她抬起自己的双臂，优雅的双脚滑过地板。小美人鱼的舞蹈很迷人，她让所有人都着了迷，特别是王子。
But as the Little Mermaid listened, she was surprised that she wanted to dance. She lifted her arms, and slid her graceful feet across the floor. The Little Mermaid danced magically, and everyone, especially the Prince, was charmed.
“你太特别了。” 他说，“我会永远把你带在我身边，叫你‘我的小伙伴’。” 
小美人鱼对王子微笑。她的眼睛问：“这是你的承诺吗？” 
"You are so special," he said. "I will always keep you by my side and call you my little companion."
The Little Mermaid smiled at him. Her eyes asked, "Is that a promise?"
随着时间的过去，小美人鱼变成了王子最好的朋友。他走到哪里都带着她。他们一起走过王宫花园，骑马穿过田野。
As time went on, the Little Mermaid became the Prince’s best friend. He took her wherever he went. They walked through the palace gardens and rode on horseback through the fields.
他们一起爬上高高的山。虽然她不能跟他说话，但她一直微笑，即使她的脚疼得要命。
They climbed high mountains together. And though she could not speak to him, she kept smiling—even as her feet stung with pain.
晚上，当别人都睡着了，小美人鱼会登上王宫阳台。在那里，她看见海上的波浪，想起在海底的家人们。有一天晚上，她看见她的姐姐们在远处的海里游泳。
At night, while the others slept, the Little Mermaid went up to the palace balcony. There she watched the waves on the sea and remembered her family beneath it. One night she saw her sisters swimming far away in the sea.
“我们想念亲爱的小妹妹。” 大姐大喊。
她当然不能回答。但她想：“我也想你们。但是，我要和王子结婚，幸福地生活在地上世界。” 
"We miss our dear little sister," the oldest cried out.
Of course, the Little Mermaid could not reply. Instead she thought, "I miss you too. But I will marry my Prince and live happily in the Upper World."
当她跟姐姐们挥手时，她们认出了她。她们游近岩石。她伸出双臂，告诉她们，如果她可以，她会拥抱姐姐们。从那以后，她们几乎每晚都来给妹妹唱歌。
When she waved to her sisters, they recognized her. They swam closer to the rocks. She held out her arms to show them she would hug them if she could. From then on they came almost every night to sing to their sister.
有一次，她甚至看见了智慧人鱼。智慧人鱼已经好多年没游到过海面上了。小美人鱼的父亲——海底国王，也和奶奶一起来了。他们也朝她伸出双臂。她的奶奶好像在说：“我们想你，我亲爱的孩子。” 
Once, she even saw the Wise Mermaid. The Wise Mermaid had not been up to the surface in many years. The Little Mermaid's father, the Sea King, also came with her grandmother. They stretched out their hands to her, but they did not come as close as her sisters. Her grandmother seemed to be saying, "We miss you, our dear child."
可是，小美人鱼还是想得到灵魂，想和王子在一起。
“我喜欢和你在一起，小伙伴。” 王子经常这么说，“你对我来说很宝贵。” 可是，王子从来没说过要跟她结婚。
But the Little Mermaid wanted a soul and to be with the Prince.
"I enjoy being with you, my little friend," the Prince would say. "You are very precious to me." But he never spokes about making her his bride.
“他好像把我当朋友，但是我要和她结婚。” 小美人鱼想，“如果他和别人结婚，我就会变成波浪上的泡沫。” 她想到那样的命运，她颤抖着。
"He treats me as his friend, but I must marry him," the Little Mermaid thought. "If he marries someone else, I will turn into foam on the waves." As she shivered thought of that fate, she shook.
有时候，小美人鱼会用眼睛恳求王子：“请爱我吧，我想做你最爱的女人。” 当她那么看着他时，王子会用双臂拥抱她，像哥哥一样亲了亲她的额头。但是，小美人鱼希望这能改变。
Sometimes the Little Mermaid begged the Prince with her eyes, "Please love me. I wanted to be the woman you love the most." When she looked at him that way, the Prince would hug her in his arms. He’d kiss her lightly on her forehead like a brother. But the Little Mermaid hoped that this would change.
有一天，王后对国王说：“王子该结婚了。” 她希望王子能跟邻国的一位公主结婚。“我们安排他们见面吧。” 她告诉国王。
One day the Queen said to the King, "It is time for the Prince to marry." She hoped that he would marry a princess from a neighboring kingdom. "Let’s arrange for them to meet," she told the king.
当王子听到这个消息，他摇了摇头。“我不想跟那个公主结婚，小伙伴。” 他说，“我只会跟我真爱的人结婚。” 
然后，他给小美人鱼讲了他的故事。
When the Prince heard about this, he shook his head. "I will never marry that princess, my little friend," he said. "I will only marry the person I truly love."
Then he told the Little Mermaid his story.
“几年前，在暴风雨中，我坐的船沉了，我差点儿死了。幸好我被冲到了海边。当我睁开眼睛，我看见一个少女救了我的命。她的名字叫梅丹。她把我带到了附近的教堂。当我第二天醒过来时，她离开了。虽然我只见过她一面，但她就是我唯一的真爱。除非我找不到她，否则我绝不会结婚。” 
"A few years ago, I was on a ship that sank in a storm, and I almost died. Luckily I was swept ashore. When I opened my eyes, I saw the girl who had saved my life. Her name is Maiden. She took me to a nearby church. When I awoke the next day, she was gone. Though I saw her only once, that girl is my true love. If I cannot find her, I will never marry."
“可是，是我救了你的！” 小美人鱼想，“请你试着去理解。” 
但是，王子无法理解，只是对着她微笑。“哦，小伙伴，别因为我的故事而伤心。即使我永远也找不到她，有你在我身边，我也很幸福。” 
"But it was I who saved you!" thought the Little Mermaid. "Please try to understand."
But the Prince could not understand and only smiled at her. "Oh, my lovely little friend, do not feel sad because of my story. Even if I cannot find her, I am happy with you by my side."
“如果他知道真相该多好啊。” 她想，“总有一天，王子会明白我比其他任何人更爱他。然后，到时候他就会跟我结婚的。” 
小美人鱼不得不再忍耐一段时间。
"It would be so great if he knew the truth," she thought. "Someday the Prince will realize that I love him more than anyone does. And when the time comes, he will marry me."
She would have to be patient and wait for a little while longer.
王室准备了一艘船送王子去邻国。一艘华丽的船做好了出发的准备。
The royal family prepared a ship to take the Prince to the neighboring kingdom. The magnificent ship was ready for the voyage.
王子对小美人鱼解释这次航海。“这是我父母的希望，他们希望我见见那个公主。不过，你我都知道，我不会跟她结婚。毕竟，她不是救我命的那个女孩儿。”

第二天，王国卫兵们骑着马宣布婚礼。

然后，王室成员们行进穿过城里，一直走到中心广场。在那里，王子和梅丹公主手拉手结了婚。他们接吻时，人群欢呼起来。

满天放着烟花。船员和客人们一起随着音乐跳舞。
王子走向小美人鱼，鞠躬问：“我的小伙伴，你能为我的客人跳舞吗？” 
Fireworks filled the sky. Sailors and guests danced together to the music.
