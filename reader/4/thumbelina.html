“我希望我能有个孩子。” 她叹息道。
A long long time ago, there lived a kind woman. She loved to keep busy in the garden. Her name was Marigold, and she lived a simple life. She had only one wish.
"I wish that I had a child," she sighed.
一天早上，她很早就起来了。她往窗外看，发现了一件奇怪的事情。
“为什么我的花朵摇摆得这么厉害？” 她说。她走到外面去看个究竟。

"Why are my flowers shaking so hard?" she said. She went outside to have a better look.
“天啊！这是什么？” 她吓了一跳。
